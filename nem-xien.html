Instruction

You can adjust the angle of the cannon relative to the horizontal. The muzzle velocity can also be adjusted. A cursor is provided to enable the making of measurements. Air resistance is ignored.

Click and tilt the cannon.

Click on a bullet to plot the energy at this point.

Click on Play to fire.
